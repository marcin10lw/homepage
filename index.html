About

My name is Martin Augun, I'm 23 years old, and I've been living in Gdańsk for over 7 years. I graduated from a technical school in the field of Electronics Technician. I am currently studying economics at the Faculty of Economics of the University of Gdańsk. Before I get to my interests, I will explain why I am here.

YouCode

The last two years have been a great time for self-reflection for me. I have set myself some goals and priorities that I want to stick to. Studies do not give me satisfaction and a sense of self-realization, so I decided to take a dean's leave, learn programming with the help of the YouCode online frontend school and at the same time start developing my own business, but more on that in a moment.

Hobbies

Ever since my mother enrolled 7-year-old me in a music school, it was music that began to play one of the most important roles in my life.
The guitar was, is, and probably will be the main instrument I play, but other instruments are not alien to me.

Even though the music was very important to me until 2018, I didn't know much about the technical side of music. It was in 2018 that I began to be interested in music production, i.e. how a song is created, and what path it goes through.

I currently have a small recording/production studio called Dissonance Studio that I built a few months ago, where I make artist-ready music.
